Summer Swim Camps

Building Swimming Skills All Summer Long

If your child is between the ages of 5 to 14 and can swim two lengths across a 25-yard pool unassisted, consider enrolling them in our summer swim camps. Discounts are also available for students who enroll in three sessions or more.

Students will work on stroke technique, breathing, starts and turns, and speed work. Mid-day snacks and beverages are provided. Swim games will be run during the last 30-45 minutes of each session.
